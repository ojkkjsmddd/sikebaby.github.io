Culture of Nepal
INTRODUCTION

The culture of Nepal is a rich tapestry woven with ancient traditions, diverse ethnic groups, and a blend of Hinduism and Buddhism. It encompasses various aspects of life, including art, music, dance, language, religion, and social customs.
